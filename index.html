About Us

At the Carson College of Business, we empower students to become successful leaders through hands-on learning and global experiences. Our practical curriculum, featuring real-world simulations, collaborations with corporate partners, and faculty-led study abroad programs, prepares you for today's dynamic business environment. Supported by faculty research in areas like consumer behavior and supply chain management, and a community where you're more than just a number, we offer an education that's both personal and prestigious. Proudly, we're among the less than 2% of business schools worldwide fully accredited in both business and accounting by the AACSB.
